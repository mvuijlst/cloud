How to play

  1. Click a square to highlight every place that hides the same secret letter.
  2. Select a letter from the alphabet. Each guess costs 2 points, and a letter can be used only once.
  3. Lock letters when you're confident. Correct locks earn 10 points; wrong locks lose 10.
  4. Need help? Buy a random letter for 50 points to reveal and lock it automatically.
  5. Fill the entire alphabet to finish the puzzle and move to the next round.
